Context

The Deloitte Greenhouse is a network of innovation centers with 40+ locations internationally, leveraging human-centered design principles to design & facilitate executive strategy workshops for Fortune 100 clients. The Houston "Next Generation" Greenhouse is our largest Greenhouse to date, with a unique focus on tech-driven innovation. I was recruited as a founding member given my expertise in tech strategy and passion for innovation and design thinking.

My Role

As an Innovation & Experience Manager, I was responsible for leading the end-to-end discovery, design, development, and implementation of innovation solution showcases, as well as delivering compelling client experiences leveraging these solutions. To accomplish these goals, I worked with firm leadership to establish a resourcing pool of 20+ innovation-minded practitioners, and drove a scaled agile process for identifying business objectives, defining & prioritizing MVP concepts showcases to meet those objectives.

With clear objectives in mind, I leveraged my network across Deloitte's innovation ecosystem to understand common challenges our clients faced across industries, identify novel solutions, and managing a team of designers, researchers, analysts and developers to implement and iterate on solution showcases. These solution showcases were delivered in a museum gallery-like format, using state of the art display technology (touchscreens, 360 degree projection, AR/VR, magnetic prints etc.) to create visually appealing and interactive experiences to promote maximum client engagement.

As our innovation showcase inventory grew from 0 to 18+, we began leveraging those showcases as part of our client executive experiences. I became the de facto expert for delivering a compelling experience to our clients. As part of 10+ innovation-focused experiences, I led 100+ client executives through demonstrations, designed and delivered hands-on exercises, and facilitated discussion around the unique challenges their organizations faced, and how innovative technologies could be applied to address those challenges in an novel way to maximize competitive advantages.
